01 · Problem

Healthcare Delivery Is Broken in India

Six structural failures make accessing care dangerous, fragmented, and exhausting — especially at night.

🌙
Access
No Night Pharmacies
Medical emergencies don't follow business hours. Finding an open pharmacy at 2 AM is near impossible.
📦
Supply
Stock Unavailability
Patients visit 3–4 pharmacies before finding one drug. Insulin & cardiac meds often absent.
👨‍⚕️
Access
Doctor Access Gap
Good specialists booked weeks out. Patients self-medicate dangerously while waiting.
📋
Process
Prescription Friction
Verification delays and broken refill flows cause patients to miss critical doses.
💸
Economics
Price Opacity
MRP overcharging is rampant. Patients unaware of cheaper generics, overpaying 3–5×.
🔀
Experience
Fragmented Journey
5 different apps for consult, diagnose, buy, deliver, follow-up. Zero end-to-end flow.
